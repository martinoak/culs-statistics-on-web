\dr{Wilcoxon two-sample test}
\vspace*{2mm}
\newline $X_1,\dots,X_m$ independent with the same distribution, $Y_1,\dots,Y_n$ independent with the same distribution, independent samples
\newline $H_0:$ $X_k$ and $Y_k$ have the same distribution
\newline
postup:
\newline - we associate the corresponding orders with values $X_1,\dots, X_m, Y_1,\dots,Y_n$ 
\newline - $T_1=$ sum of orders of $X_k$, $T_2=$ sum of orders of  $Y_k$,
\newline - $ U_1=mn+\frac12m(m+1) -T_1 \quad  U_2=mn+\frac12n(n+1) -T_2 $
\newline $H_0$  rejected $\quad \Longleftrightarrow \quad \min(U_1,U_2) \geq k$
