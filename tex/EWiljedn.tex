\dr{Wilcoxon one-sample test}
\vspace*{2mm}
\newline $X_1,\dots,X_n$ independent with the same distribution
\newline $H_0:$ the distribution is symmetrical around $a$ ($F(a-x)+F(a+x)=1$)
\newline
postup:
\newline - we associate the corresponding order with the values $|X_1-a|,\dots, |X_n-a|$
\newline - $S^{+}=$ sum of positive $X_k-a$, $S^{-}=$ sum of negative $X_k-a$
\newline $H_0$:  rejected $\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad \min(S^{+},S^{-}) \geq k$
