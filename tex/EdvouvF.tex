\dr{two-sample F-test}
\par $X_{1},\dots,X_{m}\sim N(\mu_1,\sigma_1^2)$,  $Y_{1},\dots,Y_{n}\sim N(\mu_2,\sigma_2^2)$ nez·vislÈ

$$ \qquad   Z=\frac{S_{X}^2}{S_{Y}^2}\sim F_{m-1,n-1}     $$
\vspace*{1mm}
\par\noindent - two-tail test on level $\alpha$
\vspace*{3mm}
\par $H_0$:  $\sigma_1=\sigma_2 $ rejected $\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad$
 $ Z\leq F_{m-1,n-1}(\frac{\alpha}2)$ or $Z \geq  F_{m-1,n-1}(1-\frac{\alpha}2)$
